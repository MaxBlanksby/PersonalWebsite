About This Project

Made for a project in my Modern World history class. It was made to explore the themes of surveillance in modern society. it asks for camera and location permissions to demonstrate how pervasive surveillance technology is in today's world. read about what my and my classmates research found on the topic. Flask-based site exploring surveillance themes—Panopticon, CCTV, smart cities—with smooth scrolling and embedded media.
