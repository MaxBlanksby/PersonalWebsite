About This Project

During my summer 2025 internship at InVivo Biosystems, I contributed to the development and design of the FishCam application software. FishCam is a specialized tool used to track and collect data on zebrafish, which are crucial model organisms for studying various diseases and genetic conditions. The application enables researchers to efficiently monitor zebrafish behavior and development, providing valuable insights for biomedical research.

This internship provided me with hands-on experience in developing scientific software and working with real-world biological research applications. I collaborated with the team to design software features that enhance data collection accuracy and improve the overall user experience for researchers working with zebrafish models.
