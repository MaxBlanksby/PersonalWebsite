About This Experience

Attended the University of Chicago Tech Ventures Demo Day and engaged with early stage quantum computing startups presenting applied research and commercialization efforts. Met Bob Wold from QuantumRings, who introduced a classical simulation platform designed to execute and test quantum circuits at scale. The platform highlighted how classical infrastructure can be leveraged to accelerate quantum software development and support developers working ahead of fault tolerant hardware.
