About This Program

I had the opportunity to study with Professor Marek Perkowski at Portland State University, where I explored how to map Boolean oracle algebra to quantum gates—a key step in designing efficient quantum algorithms. Through this study, I learned how to translate classical logic functions into quantum circuits, enabling faster computation on quantum processors. I applied my programming skills in Python and Java to simulate and visualize these mappings, deepening my understanding of both classical and quantum computing. This project strengthened my technical foundation and inspired me to keep pushing the boundaries of what’s possible with quantum technology.
