About This Course

Completed an advanced online course through MIT xPRO focused on quantum algorithms and their practical applications across multiple domains. The course covered quantum algorithms specifically designed for cybersecurity applications, molecular simulation in chemistry, and optimization problems that are intractable for classical computers.

Key Topics Covered

  • Quantum algorithms for cryptography and cybersecurity
  • Quantum simulation methods for chemical systems and molecular modeling
  • Quantum optimization algorithms including QAOA and VQE
  • Practical implementations and current limitations
  • Real-world applications and use cases
