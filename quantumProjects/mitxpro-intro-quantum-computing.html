About This Course

Completed a foundational online course through MIT xPRO that provides a comprehensive introduction to quantum computing principles and technologies. The course covered fundamental concepts including quantum mechanics basics, quantum gates, quantum circuits, and introductory quantum algorithms.

Key Topics Covered

  • Quantum mechanics fundamentals: superposition and entanglement
  • Quantum gates and circuit design
  • Quantum algorithms including Deutsch-Jozsa, Grover's, and Shor's algorithms
  • Quantum hardware architectures and qubit technologies
  • Quantum error correction and noise mitigation
  • Hands-on programming with quantum computing frameworks
