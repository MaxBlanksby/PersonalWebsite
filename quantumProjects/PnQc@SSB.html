About This Program

I recently completed the Physics & Quantum Computing program at Berkeley, an intensive course introducing high school students to the fundamentals of quantum technology. Led by Albert Einstein Distinguished Fellow Mark Hannum, I studied quantum mechanics, quantum information science, and quantum hardware. Through engaging lectures and hands-on activities, I explored fundamental concepts like superposition and entanglement, gaining a deep understanding of how quantum computing can solve problems classical computers cannot. I also learned about real-world applications from industry leaders like IBM, Google, and Rigetti. Learn more about the program here.
